Projects

The Trials of Tiresias

My senior project team made The Trials of Tiresias into a First-Person RPG with missions to teach blind students the programming language, Quorum. We also added an experimental First-Person Shooter aspect for blind players.

Quorum's Accessibility Library

I made two native libraries to catch system notifications for both OS X and Windows. I then set up a new Quorum, a programing language tailored to blind students, library that creates a screen reader in as little as 20 lines of code.
